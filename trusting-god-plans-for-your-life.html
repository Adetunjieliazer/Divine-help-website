Trusting God's Plans For Your Life

The Divine Help blog. Learn to apply Bible to everyday life.

Do you trust God’s plans for your life? As we enter spring, seniors are thinking about graduation, college plans, or future careers. It is important to believe God has a plan for your future whether you are about to graduate or moving forward to the next steps in your life. This playlist has Scriptures to help us during these transitional times or when we are unsure about the future.

Advertisement

Psalm 105:3

Praise him, because his name is holy. Let the hearts of those who trust in the Lord be glad.

Psalm 112:7

They aren’t afraid when bad news comes. They stand firm because they trust in the Lord.

Psalm 115:9

All you Israelites, trust in the Lord. He helps you like a shield that keeps you safe.

Psalm 118:8

It is better to go to the Lord for safety than to trust in mere human beings. It is better to go to the Lord for safety than to trust in human leaders.

Proverbs 3:5

Trust in the Lord with all your heart. Do not depend on your own understanding.

Proverbs 28:25

People who always want more stir up conflict. But those who trust in the Lord will succeed.

Isaiah 32:17

Doing what is right will bring peace and rest. When my people do that, they will stay calm and trust in the Lord forever.

Isaiah 40:30-31

[30] Even young people become worn out and get tired. Even the best of them trip and fall. [31] But those who trust in the Lord will receive new strength. They will fly as high as eagles. They will run and not get tired. They will walk and not grow weak.

Isaiah 50:10

Does anyone among you have respect for the Lord? Does anyone obey the message of the Lord’s servant? Let the person who walks in the dark trust in the Lord. Let the one who doesn’t have any light to guide them depend on their God.

Nahum 1:7

The Lord is good. When people are in trouble, they can go to him for safety. He takes good care of those who trust in him.

2 Thessalonians 3:4

We trust in the Lord. So we are sure that you are doing the things we tell you to do. And we are sure that you will keep on doing them.
