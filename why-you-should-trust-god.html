Why You Should Trust God

The Divine Help blog. Learn to apply Bible to everyday life.

God is someone who makes big plans and promises to fulfill them. These Scriptures show us some of God’s amazing qualities and why we should trust him more.

Advertisement

Number 23:19 ERV

God is not a man; he will not lie. God is not a human being; his decisions will not change. If he says he will do something, then he will do it. If he makes a promise, then he will do what he promised.

Isaiah 55:8-9 ERV

The Lord says, “My thoughts are not like yours. Your ways are not like mine. Just as the heavens are higher than the earth, so my ways are higher than your ways, and my thoughts are higher than your thoughts.

1 Corinthians 10:13 MSG

No test or temptation that comes your way is beyond the course of what others have had to face. All you need to remember is that God will never let you down; he’ll never let you be pushed past your limit; he’ll always be there to help you come through it.

1 John 1:5 MSG

This, in essence, is the message we heard from Christ and are passing on to you: God is light, pure light; there’s not a trace of darkness in him.

Psalm 54:4 ERV

Look, my God will help me. My Lord will support me.

Psalm 48:14 NLT

For that is what God is like. He is our God forever and ever, and he will guide us until we die.

1 Corinthians 1:9 MSG

God, who got you started in this spiritual adventure, shares with us the life of his Son and our Master Jesus. He will never give up on you. Never forget that.

Psalm 68:19 NIV

Praise be to the Lord, to God our Savior, who daily bears our burdens.
