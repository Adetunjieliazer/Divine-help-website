Bible study program...

Our Bible study program start by 5pm every thursday, @ O.I.M C&S DIVINE HELP Church.

Get your Holy Bible mobile app by clicking on the Holy Bible.

Where we study the God's Word which guides us in the right direction in life. Receiving direction from God, "your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path(Psa. 119:105)". It lights the way ahead of us so we can see clearly which way to go. Through every of our life, we can be confident that God is always leading us through His Word

And strengthened your faith, "so faith comes from hearing through the word of Christ(Rom. 10:17)". Being exposed to God's Word will spar faith in you. You will know what to believe and what to base your faith on.
